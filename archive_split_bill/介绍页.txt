Page 1 — Welcome
“First, users enter the KTV mobile system.We wanted the opening screen to feel simple and comfortable.
Page 2 — Room Dashboard
“On the main page, users can see the current song and other room functions.At the bottom, there is a clear ‘Split Bill’ button.”
Page 3 — Select Participants
“Users can choose who will join the payment.This helps make the bill more flexible for different group situations.”
Page 4 — Bill Overview
“This page shows the total bill, payment details, and split options.Users can choose Equal Split or Custom Split.”
Page 5 — Custom Split
“In Custom Split, users can change how much each person pays.For example, someone who spent more can pay more.”
Page 6 — Auto Calculation
“In Equal Split, the system automatically calculates the payment amount for each person.This makes the process faster and easier.”
Page 7 — Confirm & Pay
“Users can check the payment details and choose a payment method before confirming.”
Page 8 — Payment Status
“After payment, users can see the payment status of the group.The system also sends gentle reminders to unpaid users.”
Page 9 — All Settled
“Finally, when everyone has paid, the system shows a completion page and users can return to the KTV room.”
