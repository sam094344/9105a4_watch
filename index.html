1. The Design Solution

An elegant wrist-based watch companion designed specifically to bypass the auditory, cognitive, and physical challenges of high-decibel KTV entertainment environments.

Device Status Context

Round watch face displays Room 218 details and current playing song metadata.

2x2 Action Target

Oversized circular bubbles replace text links for mistap prevention under visual strain.

PPG Health Sync

Background sensors silently monitor heart rate warning spikes without user input.

2. Deepened Design Pain Points

I
Auditory Overload & Isolation

KTV rooms average 85-95 dB, washing out voice prompts and phone rings. Important system updates are completely missed.

II
Cognitive & Fine-Motor Decline

Alcohol consumption degrades coordination. Nesting menus or typing details causes high error rates.

III
Physical Stress & Silent Risks

High-energy singing mixed with alcohol spikes cardiovascular loads, frequently escaping peers' attention.

IV
Chaotic Group Coordination

Calculating food tabs under the influence generates payment delay friction and social awkwardness.

3. Key Visual Checkpoints

1. Call for Water (Tactile Request)

Issues a silent staff request, confirming with a long wrist haptic vibration and estimated delivery time.

2. Care+ PPG Monitor (Safety Net)

Integrates watch sensors to track heart rate spikes, launching peer alert gates during dangerous spikes.

3. Zero-Setup Safe Ride

One-tap ride hailing sends a cab directly to pre-registered home addresses, skipping mobile entry typing.

4. Iteration Log

Round 1: Target Grids Usability

Change: Text rows replaced with symmetrical 2x2 grid layout. Outcome: Mistaps dropped near zero.

Round 2: Buffer Screens Confirm

Change: Added "Calling..." screen with 2s hold. Outcome: Prevents accidental staff calls.

C. Heuristic Reflections
Visibility of Status

Friction: Noise makes service confirmations uncertain.

Correction: Added distinct long wrist-vibrations on callback.

Error Prevention

Friction: Motor decline leads to accidental taps.

Correction: Set 2s touch-and-hold delay buffer on triggers.

System & Real World Match

Friction: Complex payment labels are confusing under influence.

Correction: Simplified to "Total", "You Pay", and "Equal split".

Flexibility & Efficiency

Friction: Navigating nested lists is frustrating.

Correction: Implemented top-left escape shortcut back-arrows.
